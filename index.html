About Us

We aim to connect students and staff with trusted e-waste recycling centers.

How we champion

How we champion E-Waste Recycling

Connect Users to Recycling Centers

We link students and staff to trusted e-waste recycling locations. Our platform maps nearby certified centers quickly and accurately. Finding the right place to recycle responsibly has never been easier.

Educate on E-Waste Management

We raise awareness about the dangers of irresponsible e-waste disposal. Through guides, articles, and tips, we empower users to make informed choices. Education is the first step toward real environmental change.

Promote Sustainable Campus Initiatives

We encourage schools and organizations to lead e-waste recycling drives. Campaigns and events are designed to foster community engagement. Together, we build a culture that values sustainability every day.

Simplify the Recycling Process

We streamline how students and staff interact with recycling services. No more confusion or wasted time searching for certified centers. Our platform delivers clarity, speed, and confidence in every action.
